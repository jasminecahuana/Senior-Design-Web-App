What is R0?

R0 is a mathematical term that is used to indicate how infectious a disease is. An R0 of a certain value tells the average amount of people that will contract the disease from someone who has it. For example, a disease with an R0 of 10 means the infectious disease will be transmitted to an average of 10 people. (Source)
